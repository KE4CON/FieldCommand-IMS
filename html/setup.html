📋 Default ICS Form Variant

Select your agency's standard form variant. This sets the default when creating any ICS form. You can override this per-incident or per-form at any time — forms can be mixed and matched.

Note: Not all forms have USCG or NWCG variants — those forms will use FEMA format regardless. At time of form creation you can always choose a different variant for that specific form.

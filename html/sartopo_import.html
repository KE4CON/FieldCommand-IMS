Export from SARTopo / CalTopo

In SARTopo or CalTopo, go to Menu → Export → GeoJSON. Export your map or select specific folders/features to export. All feature types are supported: polygons (search segments, areas), lines (routes, roads), and points (assignments, staging areas, hazards).
